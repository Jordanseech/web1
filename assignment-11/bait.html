Live Bait vs Artificial Bait

Choosing the right Bait can determine whether or not you catch Fish or not. It's important to know what the differences are between Live bait and Artificial bait.

How to choose Bait

Live Bait

Live bait or (Natural Bait) is anything alive that Fish eat. This could be Works,Crawfish,Leeches,or Shrimp.It all depends what type of Fish you're Fishing for and what they like. You can always switch it up if you aren't getting any bites.

Artificial Bait

Artficial Bait or (Lures) are spinners,sponns, and jigs.Lures spotted by Fish as natural foods such as Minnows, Flies, Crawdad, and Small Fish. Lures can be fun because it provides the Angler (Fisherman) to provide all the motion in tricking the Fish into thinking it' alive. Fishing Lures come in differn't styles, colors, patterns and sizes.
