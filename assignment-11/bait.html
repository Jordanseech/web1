Live Bait vs Artificial Bait

Choosing the right Bait can determine whether or not you catch Fish or not. It's important to know what the differences are between Live bait and Artificial bait.

How to choose Bait

Live Bait

Live bait or (natural) bait is anything alive, or once alive that Fish eat. This could be Worms, Leeches, Crawfish, or Shrimp. It all depends what Fish are feeling on the day, you can always switch it up if you arenâ€™t catching anything.

Artificial Bait

Artifical bait or (lures) are Spinners, Spoons, and Kastmasters. Lures are seen by Fish as natural foods such as Minnows or Flies. Lures can be more fun because it provides the angler (fisherman) to provide all the motion in tricking the Fish. Fishing Lures come in all sizes, colors, patterns, and styles.
