Beginners guide to Fishing

Fishing is the perfect excuse to get outdoors and enjoy mother nature. It's not hard to learn how to Fish, but it takes time and practice. You will learn important information and tips in each section to help you catch a Fish!

How to Cast and Reel in a Fish...

How to tie a clinch knot

  1. Thread leader (Fishing line) through the hole on the hook
  2. Twist the leader around the line upwards 7 times
  3. Now, put the leader end back through the opening near the hole on the hook.

How to cast out

  1. Hold your Fishing pole (Rod) above your waist and hold it tight. Release, your Reel and place your pole over your shoulder.
  2. Hold onto the line with your pointer finger, release and throw in forward motion.
  3. The weight of the lure pulls line off the Reel. Close the bail with your hand and you can retrieve back towards you.

How to reel in fish

  1. Keep the Fishing Rod at a 45 degree angle after you cast out.
  2. Once your Fish slows down and stops pulling start Reeling in slowly towards you.
  3. Next, pull the Rod upwards and back, dont worry your Rod will bend but it's normal.
  4. Then continue to Reel in at a 45 degree angle, just repeat this process.
